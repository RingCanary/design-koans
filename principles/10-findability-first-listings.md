# Findability-First Listings

- Status: draft
- Domain: Shared
- Claim: A-22
- Family: composition
- Lens: hierarchy
- Question: What makes a listing page useful beyond visual polish?
- Rule: Surface category, sort, and filter structures that support scanning, narrowing, and comparison.
- Why it works: On listing pages, the core task is finding the right option fast.
- Use when: Service directories, product lists, search results, admin queues, and catalog views.
- Avoid when: Cards are decorative but hide the attributes needed for comparison.
- Small example: Filter groups, sort controls, and cards expose contact, rating, availability, and category at a glance.
