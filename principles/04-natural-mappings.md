# Natural Mappings

- Status: draft
- Domain: Shared
- Claim: A-05
- Family: affordance
- Lens: signifiers
- Question: Do controls map naturally to the effect users expect?
- Rule: Match controls to outcomes using expected cultural or physical mappings.
- Why it works: Natural mappings reduce interpretation cost and lower error rates.
- Use when: Sliders, toggles, sort controls, navigation direction, and spatial actions.
- Avoid when: Iconography or directionality reverses the user’s mental model.
- Small example: Increasing values move right or up; sort indicators align with the resulting order.
