# Visible Focus

- Status: draft
- Domain: Shared
- Claim: A-19
- Family: quality
- Lens: accessibility
- Question: Can keyboard users always tell where focus is?
- Rule: Provide visible, consistent focus indication for all focusable controls and keep it unobscured.
- Why it works: Keyboard navigation depends on a stable, visible anchor point.
- Use when: Any screen includes focusable controls, overlays, or asynchronous layout changes.
- Avoid when: Focus rings are removed, too low-contrast, or hidden by transforms and clipping.
- Small example: A high-contrast outline remains visible through hover, pressed, and validation states.
