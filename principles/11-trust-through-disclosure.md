# Trust Through Disclosure

- Status: draft
- Domain: Shared
- Claim: A-23
- Family: affordance
- Lens: trust
- Question: How does a screen signal credibility without relying on surface polish?
- Rule: Design trust with disclosure, currentness, and verifiable signals alongside visual quality.
- Why it works: Credibility depends on evidence, transparency, and freshness, not just visual finish.
- Use when: Listings, provider cards, pricing pages, dashboards, and any decision-critical screen.
- Avoid when: Trust cues are purely decorative or stale.
- Small example: A card shows last-updated date, pricing clarity, verification status, and review freshness.
